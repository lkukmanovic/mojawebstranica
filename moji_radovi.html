Special recognition award

This book was created on first MA year, on course “Post digital publishing” under the hand of my mentor doc. Igor Kuduz. It deals with the possibilities of coexistence of old and new technologies. The new era promised us that everything we have on our smart devices and computers will be forever in that "wild world of internet". But, is that really true? What if everything we have, all those photos, emails, messages and datas just went gone? So, this book also represents the fear of irretrievable loss of one of those informations, “ a good night messages” from my grandfather, whose loss would be terrible and unbearable.

So, this book is made of a "good night messages" that my grandfather Rudi sent me, and my answers to him. They are made in spring of 2018. Later that year, in September 2018., my project “Rudi wishes You Good Night” had won an Special recognition award, which I am very proud of.
